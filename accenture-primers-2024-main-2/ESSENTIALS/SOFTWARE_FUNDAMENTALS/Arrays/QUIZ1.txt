1. A farmer has 17 sheep and all but nine die. How many are left?

Select one: 9

2. In a year, there are 12 months. Seven months have 31 days. How many months have 28 days?

Select one: 12

3. What is the next  letter in the following sequence? J,F, M, A, M, J, J, A, __.

Select one: S

The sequence is first letter of the months of the year. September is the next in the sequence.
